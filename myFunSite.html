About us

Grocery Shop Today is founded in 2021 during the pandemic. It is used to serve users who do not want to leave their houses to purchase groceries.

We will provide free delivery of the freshest vegetables and meat you will ever get in the States.

Food Quality

Our vegetables are self planted without the use of chemicals.

Our meat are all organic as well!

Where does our food come from?

We only provide the freshest foods. They are shipped from all over the world. Each type of food is shipped from the place where it best grows.
